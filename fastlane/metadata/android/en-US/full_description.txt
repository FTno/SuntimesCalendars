This add-on for Suntimes supplies the Calendar app with calendars and events (moon phases, solstices, and equinoxes).

Permissions are required for features to work properly:
* WRITE_SYNC_SETTINGS (READ_SYNC_STATS) is used to create and add custom calendars to the Calendar app.
* WRITE_CALENDAR (READ_CALENDAR) is used to populate custom calendars with events.
